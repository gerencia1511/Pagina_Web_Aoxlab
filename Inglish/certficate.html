What is Product Certification?

ISO/IEC 17065:2012 certification evaluates that products, processes or services comply with specific standards of quality, safety and sustainability, providing confidence to all stakeholders

Independent Evaluation

Impartial validation performed by accredited third parties that confirms compliance with national and international regulations, guaranteeing objectivity throughout the process.

Global Recognition

Internationally recognized certificates that facilitate access to global markets and generate consumer confidence, improving your company's competitiveness.

Quality Guarantee

Objective demonstration that your products meet specific standards, which increases consumer confidence and reduces quality-related risks.

Our Policies

Commitments that guarantee the integrity of our certification processes

Confidentiality Policy

At Aoxlab we are committed to safeguarding all information provided by our clients during the certification process. All our staff, including auditors, technical experts and committee members, sign confidentiality agreements that guarantee the protection of your information.

  • Absolute protection of confidential information and sensitive data
  • Use of information exclusively for certification purposes
  • Prohibition of disclosure to third parties without written authorization
  • Security systems to protect physical and digital documentation

Data Processing Policy

We strictly comply with current legislation on personal data protection. Your information will be processed only for purposes related to the certification process and under your explicit consent.

  • Data collection with informed consent
  • Secure storage with advanced technical measures
  • Restricted access only to authorized personnel
  • Right to access, rectification and deletion of data

Impartiality Policy

We guarantee objectivity and independence in all our certification processes. Our decisions are based exclusively on objective evidence, free from commercial, financial or other influences.

  • Independent evaluation of all products
  • Clear separation between consulting and certification activities
  • Impartiality committee with balanced representation
  • Identification and management of conflicts of interest

Non-Discrimination Policy

Our certification services are available to all organizations that request them, without discrimination by size, sector, affiliation to associations or groups, or number of certifications already issued.

  • Equitable access to certification services
  • Objective and transparent evaluation criteria
  • Fair rates based on objective criteria
  • Equal treatment regardless of origin, size or nature
