Shelf Life Studies

We help companies accurately determine the shelf life of their products, ensuring quality, safety, and regulatory compliance throughout commercialization.

We design and execute customized studies based on the type of food, cosmetic, supplement, or natural product under real or accelerated conditions, using controlled-stability chambers with real-time telemetry and expert technical support.

What does our service include?

  • Study design according to regulations (INVIMA, Codex, FDA, etc.) and real or accelerated storage conditions
  • Stability chambers with temperature and humidity control, monitored 24/7 with real-time telemetry
  • Technical advice on labeling and defining expiration or best-before dates
  • Definition of critical points and selection of key variables (temperature, humidity, light, etc.)
  • Periodic sampling during the study as per the defined plan
  • Physico-chemical, microbiological, sensory, or functional analyses, depending on product category
  • Mathematical prediction models (Arrhenius, Q10, etc.) for accelerated studies
  • Interpretation of results and delivery of a comprehensive technical report with final recommendations
  • Support in sanitary registration processes or audits if required

Applicable products

Processed, fresh, or frozen foods Beverages Cosmetics and personal care products Dietary supplements Natural and phytotherapeutic products

What sets Aoxlab apart?

  • Proprietary chambers with real-time telemetry and continuous data backup
  • Multisector experience: food, cosmetics, cannabis, supplements, etc.
  • Experts in experimental design and international regulations
  • Clear, auditable reports ready for regulatory procedures

Accredited Biodegradability Studies

Accredited service under ISO/IEC 17025:2017

We conduct accredited analyses to evaluate the biodegradability of products designed as alternatives to single-use plastics, under international standards such as ASTM D5338.

Our study allows companies to demonstrate with technical evidence and traceability that their products truly degrade under controlled conditions, facilitating regulatory compliance, certification processes, and differentiation in sustainable markets.

What does our accredited service include?

  • Study protocol design according to ASTM D5338 (aerobic biodegradation in composting)
  • Execution in composting chambers with temperature and humidity control under standardized conditions
  • Real-time telemetry: continuous monitoring of temperature, humidity, and COâ‚‚ release
  • Positive and negative controls as required by the standard to ensure test validity
  • Technical monitoring throughout the study
  • Data analysis and cumulative biodegradation curve
  • Delivery of a legally valid technical report with traceability, useful for certifications and procedures before authorities
  • Scientific advice to interpret results and communicate the productâ€™s environmental impact

Who is it for?

  • Packaging, food, or cosmetics companies developing compostable or biodegradable products
  • Manufacturers of straws, bags, cups, cutlery, and sustainable films
  • Innovation projects in bioplastics, biomaterials, or circular economy
  • Brands seeking environmental certifications, complying with the Single-Use Plastics Law, and accessing conscious markets

Why is accredited analysis key?
