Laboratory Analyst

Schedule

Monday to Friday (1 Saturday per month)

  • At least 1 year of experience in physicochemical analysis
  • Handling of laboratory equipment and quality training (ISO/IEC 17025:2017)
  • Attention to detail, organization and meeting deadlines

Benefits

  • Competitive salary
  • Training & professional development
  • Dynamic, collaborative environment
  • Wellness & recognition programs

Instrumental Analyst – Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS)

Full Time Fixed / Permanent Medellín – On-site

Schedule

Monday to Friday (1 Saturday per month)

Requirements

  • Degree in Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Chemistry or related fields
  • Minimum 2 years’ experience in instrumental analysis, especially GC-MS
  • Knowledge of method validation, spectrum interpretation and Shimadzu / similar equipment
  • Desirable: pesticide residue or VOC analysis experience
  • Analytical skills, organization and rigorous documentation

Benefits

  • Competitive salary based on experience
  • Technical training in state-of-the-art technologies
  • Stability within an innovative, multidisciplinary team
  • Wellness & recognition programs

Sample Collector (own motorcycle)

Full Time Field Work Medellín – Field & HQ

Schedule

Monday to Friday (1 Saturday per month)

Requirements

  • Technician / technologist in Chemistry, Environmental, Food or related areas
  • At least 6 months’ sampling experience (water, food, surfaces, etc.)
  • Own motorcycle with valid papers and license

  • Excellent service attitude, punctuality and organization

  • Positive work environment & stability
  • Wellness & recognition programs

SENA Apprentice

Full Time Apprenticeship Contract Medellín - On-site

Schedule

Monday to Friday (1 Saturday per month)

To start the productive stage in OCTOBER 2025.

Requirements

  • Active SENA student in programs such as Industrial Chemistry Technology, Chemical Samples Technician, or related fields.
  • Interest in homogenization processes and sample management.
  • Responsibility, orderliness, and compliance with safety and biosecurity protocols.
  • Teamwork skills and a willingness to learn.

Responsibilities

  • Support in the reception, registration, and storage of samples according to internal protocols.
  • Execution of sample homogenization tasks under the supervision of technical staff.
  • Compliance with quality procedures related to sample preservation and traceability.
  • Collaboration in organization, labeling, and inventory control tasks.

Benefits

  • 1 SMLV (minimum legal wage) with all legal benefits.
  • Practical training in an ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accredited laboratory.
  • Guidance and mentorship from professionals in the field.
  • Development of skills in key quality and sample management processes.
  • Opportunity for a permanent position based on performance.
